# Retail Sales Power BI Dashboard

This is a Power BI dashboard I built to look at retail sales across U.S. states from 2015 to 2018.

## Dashboard Preview

![Dashboard Preview](dashboard.png)

## About the project

The goal of this dashboard is to give a quick view of sales performance and make it easy to explore the data by state, segment, product category, and time.

It helps answer questions like:
- how much total sales were made
- which states sold the most
- which customer segment performed best
- which sub-categories brought in the most sales
- how sales changed over time

## What’s in the dashboard

- **YoY Growth %**  
  shows year-over-year sales growth

- **Avg Order Value**  
  shows the average amount spent per order

- **Total Sales**  
  shows total sales in the selected view

- **Sales by U.S. State & Region**  
  map view of sales across the U.S.

- **Total Sales by Segment**  
  compares Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office

- **Total Sales by Sub-Category**  
  shows which product groups sold the most

- **Monthly Sales Trend (2015–2018)**  
  shows how sales changed over time

## Filters

This dashboard includes filters for:
- sub-category
- year

## Main takeaways

- sales generally increased over time
- the Consumer segment had the highest sales
- Phones and Chairs were among the top sub-categories
- some states like California and New York stood out more than others
- the selected view shows 20.30% YoY growth
